What is a die cut?
• "Die Cut" is the technical term for a custom, cut to size piece of tape
• The cut is the tape, and the die is the tool that does the cutting
• Die cut parts greatly improve application efficiency and lowers cost by reducing waste
Die Cut parts can be used to meet specific application needs
